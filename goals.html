Goals

Short Term Goals (Next 1-2 Years)

  • Gain hands-on experience in IT and data management through rotations and projects.
  • Strengthen technical skills in programming, databases, and cybersecurity fundamentals.
  • Improve communication and teamwork abilities by collaborating across departments.
  • Earn industry-recognized certifications and training to validate technical expertise.

Mid Term Goals (Next 3-5 Years)

  • Advance into a full-time role in IT, software engineering, or data management.
  • Take on leadership opportunities such as mentoring interns or leading small projects.t
  • Contribute to process improvements that increase efficiency and security within an organization.
  • Broaden expertise across multiple areas of IT to become more versatile and adaptable.

Long Term Goals (Next 5-10 Years)

  • Become a trusted leader in IT or data management, known for innovative problem-solving.
  • Lead cross-functional teams and contribute to strategic decision-making at the organizational level.
  • Develop a strong professional brand by publishing insights, presenting at conferences, or mentoring others in the field.
  • Continue lifelong learning to stay current with emerging technologies and industry best practices.
